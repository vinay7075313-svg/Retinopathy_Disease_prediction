Pipeline

How It Works

Five-stage notebook pipeline — raw floats to interpretable probability via Flask + scikit-learn.

01

Enter 4 Float Values

Age, systolic BP, diastolic BP, cholesterol — decimal precision accepted.

02

Feature Engineering (Cell 25)

7 derived features auto-computed: pulse_pressure, bp_ratio, age_systolic, chol_age_ratio, hypertension_score, high_cholesterol, age_group_enc.

03

POST /predict → Flask

Browser sends 4 base values as JSON. Flask re-engineers all 11 features server-side with run_inference().

04

StandardScaler + LogisticRegression

11 features scaled by training-fitted scaler, passed to GridSearchCV-tuned LR from models/dp1_model.pkl.

05

Result + Clinical Guidance

Probability [0–1] mapped to 4-tier classification with consult alert and diet plan.

Risk Classification

Four-Tier Risk System

The model probability [0–1] maps to four clinically actionable tiers.

Tier 1
Minimal Risk
0 – 25%

Biomarkers within normal limits. Routine annual check-up sufficient.

Tier 2
Low Risk
25 – 55%

Slightly elevated markers. Semi-annual monitoring with lifestyle adjustments.

Tier 3
Moderate Risk
55 – 80%

Multiple elevated markers. Ophthalmology referral advised within 4 weeks.

Tier 4
High Risk
80 – 100%

Critical profile. Immediate ophthalmological examination required.
